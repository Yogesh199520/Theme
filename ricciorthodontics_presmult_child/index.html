Your Orthodontist In Johns Creek, GA and Surrounding Areas

Steven C. Ricci DDS, MS – Welcome to Ricci Orthodontics

Welcome to Ricci Orthodontics, where we offer customized orthodontic care for the whole family. Dr. Steven Ricci and our team of specialists are pleased to provide comprehensive orthodontics in Johns Creek, Georgia. We are happy to also welcome patients from nearby areas, including Atlanta, Elkridge, Peekskill, Alpharetta, Duluth, Suwanee, Cumming, Norcross, Peachtree Corners, Buford, Sugar Hill, and Rockport. Our skilled orthodontist will provide you with the right solutions to help your smile shine. We utilize technology such as digital X-rays, cold laser therapy, TADS, and self-ligating braces. Our paperless office helps us provide the most efficient service to our patients. Ricci Orthodontics invites you to visit our fun, spa-like orthodontics office soon and experience the high-quality treatment we have provided for over 16 years. Contact us today to make an appointment for yourself or a family member!
